Quick help

My nearby restaurants are missing

Open iOS Settings, find Big Beautiful, and allow Location access “While Using the App.” You can always search by name or use the manual-place option without location access.

Photos do not appear in Backfill

The standard photo picker works without full-library permission. The optional date-range scanner needs Photos read access. Review Big Beautiful’s Photos permission in iOS Settings and choose the date range again.

My shared circle is not syncing

Confirm that iCloud Drive is enabled, both people are signed into iCloud, the invitation was accepted, and both iPhones have a network connection. CloudKit updates can take a short time to arrive. Do not post an invitation link in a public support request.

A restaurant appears twice

Open More, choose Merge Duplicate Locations, select the duplicate and the record you want to keep, then review the merge before confirming.

I need to correct or remove a meal

Open History, select the visit, and use the visit-detail controls. Deleting a visit removes its attached ratings, dish entries, and app-managed photo copies.

Privacy choices

Big Beautiful has no developer account or developer-held copy of your ledger. Your privacy controls live on your iPhone and in your Apple Account:

  1. Manage Location and Photos permissions in iOS Settings for Big Beautiful.
  2. Delete an individual visit from its detail screen in the app.
  3. Manage shared-circle participants using Apple’s sharing controls.
  4. Manage app data stored in iCloud through your Apple Account’s iCloud storage settings.
