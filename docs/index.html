Best Restaurant Ranking App in the World

Every meal deserves a place in history.

Big Beautiful Restaurant Log is a private, native iPhone app for logging meals in three taps, remembering what mattered, and building restaurant rankings that become more useful with every visit.

iPhone only. Private by design. No ads, analytics, feeds, or developer account.

01 · Remember

Log in three taps

Choose the place, capture your reaction, and move on. Add dishes, photos, company, and memories only when you want to.

02 · Rank

A living table

Rankings balance reactions, direct comparisons, certainty, and recency instead of pretending one permanent grade can tell the whole story.

03 · Share

Your private circle

Keep a personal log or invite someone through Apple’s private CloudKit sharing. Each person keeps an independent point of view.

Privacy is a feature

Your memories belong to you.

There is no developer-operated server, advertising, behavioral analytics, or tracking. Your Big Beautiful Log stays on your iPhone and, when enabled, in your own private or explicitly shared iCloud database.
